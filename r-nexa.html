Advanced Features of ROTAI R-NEXA Stretch Massage Chair

Dual-Core Synergy Mechanism

The R-NEXA features an exclusive Dual-Core Synergy Quad-Hand Massage system with two independent sets of rollers working simultaneously delivering 100% higher efficiency compared to single-core massage systems. The Upper 4D SmartSync Core handles neck-to-back precision, while the Lower 3D Hip Mechanism provides 24 cm ultra-wide independent hip and lower-body coverage

Golden Angle Flexible Track

Precision-engineered with a golden semi-reclined angle, the FlexTrack evenly distributes body pressure across the spine and delivers an intense muscle-stretching sensation. R-Nexa specially designed with 4D massage technology with a reclining range of up to 160° and a flexible guide rail pressure maintained at ≤30 mmHg, the R-NEXA ensures a smooth, pressure-free full-body stretch significantly lower than standard supine position pressure.

Bi-Stretch² Stretching System

The R-NEXA’s exclusive Bi-Stretch² System enables independent leg movement for deeper lower-body stretching and enhanced flexibility. This advanced massage chair with 4D massager technology integrates four professional techniques Iliopsoas Stretching, PNF Stretching, Full-Body Stretching, and Recovery Stretching into one seamless dual-stretch experience, delivering exceptional comfort and deeply therapeutic results.

Hall Sensor Body Detection- Faster and More Accurate

Powered by intelligent Hall Sensor technology, the R-NEXA's motorized roller tracks your body movement and uses advanced algorithms to map your entire body accurately. Featuring ROVO Walking Technology for advanced full body massage performance, it automatically adjusts pressure and massage techniques based on detected body zones, preventing misaligned massage. Compatible with users from 1500 mm to 1900 mm in height for a fully personalized fit every time.

38-Airbag Full Body Air Pressure Massage

A total of 38 strategically placed airbags from shoulders to feet are powered by a high-performance air pump, relaxing muscles through controlled inflation and deflation that simulates gentle hand pressure. Integrated heating in the waist, knees, and legs adds therapeutic warmth, designed for full body relaxation with advanced electric massage chair functionality, covering shoulder airbags, arm airbags, waist heating, leg airbags, knee and leg heating, and foot airbags for complete head-to-toe compression care.
