4D PRO Body Shape Detection

Dual Smart Controls

Anti-Pinch Sensor

Premium Leather Coating

Crafted with breathable, durable leather available in multiple colors, the chair ensures long-lasting comfort and style. This premium finish enhances the luxurious feel, making it one of the most visually appealing and Premium Massage Chairs in the market.

39 Intelligent Programs

ROVO Mode

ROVO Mode offers specialized routines for hamstrings, iliopsoas, piriformis, twisting, PNF stretching, Energize, Refresh and Cycling. This mode is ideal for those needing flexibility training and deep recovery, providing an advanced Rovo Walking Massage Chair-style personalized experience.

Health Massage

Auto Modes
