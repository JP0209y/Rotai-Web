About Us

History of Massage Chairs

1997 The Beginning

Rotai was founded with a vision to bring health, wellness, and comfort into every home.

Initially focused on small-scale massagers and wellness devices.

2000 First Generation Massage Chairs

Rotai introduced its first massage chair models, designed for relaxation and basic back massage.

Established itself as a rising name in the wellness technology industry.

2005 Expansion & Innovation

Launched multi-functional massage chairs with improved ergonomics.

Began expanding globally, reaching customers outside of China.

2010 Breakthrough in Technology

Introduced zero-gravity massage technology, inspired by NASA.

Advanced 3D roller systems were integrated for a deeper massage experience.

2015 Smart Wellness Era

Launch of intelligent massage chairs with remote control and customized programs.

Expanded product categories to include foot massagers, handheld devices, and wellness accessories.

2018 AI & Human-like Massage

Rolled out AI-powered massage chairs that adapt to the user’s body.

Focused on creating a human-like massage experience with 4D roller technology.

2020 Global Recognition

Rotai became a leading global massage chair brand, exporting to over 100+ countries.

Emphasis on wellness at home due to the pandemic increased demand.

2022 Health & Lifestyle Integration

Introduced smart app-controlled massage chairs with health tracking features.

Collaborated with wellness experts to design programs for stress relief, circulation, and sleep improvement.

2025 Continuing Innovation

Today, Rotai is known as a pioneer in massage technology, blending AI, ergonomics, and wellness science.

Their chairs are not only relaxation tools but complete health companions for families worldwide.

Our Brand

Rotai™ is the world’s most prestigious robotic massage chair company, renowned for manufacturing high-quality massage chairs. Established in 1997, Rotai™ has grown over the past twenty-five years into one of the most important manufacturers globally, focusing on robotic massage chairs. Our massage chairs are known for their exceptional quality and innovative features, reflecting our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Company Profile

ROTAI™, a leading brand in the massage equipment industry, has over 25 years of experience. As a pioneer in this field, ROTAI™ has partnered with universities and research institutions, combining advanced technology with expert engineering to improve and innovate its products. This strong focus on quality and development has earned ROTAI™ a trusted reputation and a leading position in the health and wellness market.

On November 1, 2017, ROTAI was officially listed on the A-share market under the name ROTAI HEALTH (Stock Code: 603579).
