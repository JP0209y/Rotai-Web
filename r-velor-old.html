Premium Features of the R-VELOR 4D Japanese Massage Chair

Independent Leg Movement

This advanced engineering allows each leg section to move independently, supporting core muscle stretching, leg flexibility and targeted relief. This feature makes R-VELOR feel like a premium Japanese massage chair and is ideal for users seeking the best massage chair in India.

4D Temperature-Sensing Core

The 4D temperature-sensing core enhances your full body massage chair experience with deep infrared warmth, warming tissues from neck to feet. This technology mimics authentic Japanese massage chair techniques and is perfect for users who want relaxing, therapeutic massage chairs.

Extra-Long L Track

The extra-long L Track follows natural spinal curves, offering 130°–155° reclining for deeper stretching. This track design makes the chair function as one of the best massage chairs in India, delivering consistent neck-to-hip coverage and full-body elongation and comfort.

Millimeter-Level 4D Mechanism

This precision 4D mechanism scans the body and adjusts to millimeter accuracy, focusing on neck, shoulders, back and hips. It delivers therapeutic depth similar to Japanese massage chairs while offering upgraded comfort for those wanting a high-end full body massage chair.

ROVO-Walking Technology

Inspired by Pilates and Yoga, this feature provides dynamic, muscle-focused stretching for complete leg recovery. R-VELOR enhances flexibility by simulating controlled walking movements, making this massage chair one of the most effective options for rejuvenation and athletic recovery in India.

Advanced 4D Full Body Massage Technology

Comprehensive Leg & Foot Care

The chair’s 28 airbags, triple sole rollers, and multi-zone heating deliver exceptional leg and foot therapy. With an 18cm extending leg rest, it competes with top Japanese massage chairs and provides the best massage chair experience for users in India.

Airbags Covering Nine Key Areas

Strategically positioned airbags offer rhythmic, palm-like compression from shoulders to feet. They relax the entire body, reduce stiffness, and enhance circulation, making this full body massage chair ideal for anyone seeking deep relaxation and premium massage chairs with advanced air massage systems.

Warming Heat

The chair’s infrared heating system warms the back, feet and calves up to 50°C, supporting muscle recovery and deep relaxation. This feature makes it a top choice among massage chairs for users wanting therapeutic heat, similar to high-end Japanese massage chair technology.

4D PRO Body Shape Detection

This intelligent detection system scans each user’s height, width and curve structure to personalize every massage session. R-VELOR ensures accurate depth, perfect pressure and tailored comfort, helping this model stand out as one of the best massage chairs in India.

Dual Smart Controls

Featuring both an HD touchscreen and armrest quick buttons, the chair offers seamless usability for all ages. These intuitive controls make operating a full body massage chair easier, ensuring instant access to programs, comfort levels and relaxing Japanese-style massage experiences.

Bluetooth Speakers

Built-in stereo Bluetooth speakers enhance relaxation by allowing users to enjoy music, meditation tracks or ambient sounds. This feature transforms the chair into a complete wellness zone, making it one of the most enjoyable and immersive massage chairs available in India.

Anti-Pinch Sensor

With 15 safety sensors covering leg and angle movements, this chair prevents accidental pinches and ensures safe operation. These safety elements are essential for modern families, keeping it reliable among full body massage chairs with advanced user protection.

Premium Leather Coating

Crafted with breathable, durable leather available in multiple colors, the chair ensures long-lasting comfort and style. This premium finish enhances the luxurious feel, making it one of the most visually appealing and premium Japanese-inspired massage chairs in the market.

39 Intelligent Programs

These automatic programs cover stretching, recovery, deep relaxation and targeted muscle relief, giving the feeling of using a professional therapeutic system at home. It enhances the performance expected from the best massage chair in India with diverse wellness options

ROVO Mode

ROVO Mode offers specialized routines for hamstrings, iliopsoas, piriformis, twisting, PNF stretching, Energize, Refresh and Cycling. This mode is ideal for those needing flexibility training and deep recovery, providing an advanced Japanese massage chair-style personalized experience.

Health Massage

This function improves blood circulation, reduces swelling and targets specific muscle groups such as head, hips and lower back. Its therapeutic benefits make it a complete health companion and an excellent option for users wanting a full body massage chair with medical-grade features.

Auto Modes

Auto modes include stretching, acupressure and tailored routines for CEOs, seniors, mothers, students and daily workers. These programs make the R-VELOR massage chair suitable for all families, delivering a highly customizable experience like premium Japanese massage chairs.

Massage Chair with Cycling Exercises

This specialized massage chair with cycling mode allows gentle leg cycling, boosting circulation, reducing stiffness and promoting muscle relaxation. Combined with full body stretching, it positions the chair among the best massage chairs in India for both fitness and wellness needs.
