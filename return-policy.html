About Returns and Exchanges

This site is not subject to the cooling-off (unconditional cancellation) stipulated by the INDIAN Act of Specified Commercial Transactions. Cooling off is not available.

We accept returns and exchanges in the following cases.

  • Principlly We Do Not Offer Any Return Or Exchange Scheme.
  • If the product has initial defects or defects, or if the product is damaged during delivery, the customer should notify us within 24 TO 48 HOURS after the product arrives, and we will check the operation of the product.
  • When it is clearly due to our mistake such as product difference

Return / Exchange Procedure

Please contact us within 24 TO 48 HOURS from the delivery date using the inquiry form or our toll-free number (+91-97999-79739). We will inform you about the procedure if it corresponds to the return or exchange specified by us.

Refunds

No refund fees will be charged to the customer.

  • When paying by credit card, Rakuten Pay, Amazon Pay, PayPay

After confirming the arrival of the returned product, the credit card charge will be cancelled. If your credit card company crosses the deadline, the refund will be the next withdrawal date.

  • When paying by bank transfer, cash on delivery, or convenience store payment After confirming the arrival of the returned product, the product will be refunded to your account. (It may take 2 to 3 weeks due to administrative procedures)

However, we cannot accept returns or exchanges due to customer’s convenience.

  • Products that have been used once
  • Products that do not fit the size and comfort of the product
  • Products that are marked as non-returnable at the time of sale,

etc. The cost will be borne by the customer.

About loading and unloading massage chairs

Basic delivery installation is free.

Delivery installation up to the second floor, with an elevator, no additional staff required, no crane lifting required, etc. are free of charge.

Precautions for delivery Please check the opening width of the aisle, door, etc. to the delivery location in advance.

As a guide, the width required for the delivery route is the product width + 2 cm. However, if there are L-shaped or T-shaped corners or stairs around the route from the carry-in entrance to the installation location and it is necessary to change the direction of the product, a width of 8 to 10 cm or more is required in addition to the product width. increase. Please let us know in advance when ordering the delivery location and route (such as when you do not have an elevator in an apartment and you want to bring it to the 3rd floor or above).

Additional costs may be incurred depending on the delivery method.

Delivery method that incurs additional costs

  • When delivering to the third floor or above without an elevator in condominiums, detached houses, etc.
  • When additional personnel are required
  • When lifting work with a crane is required
  • Addition If a preview (revisit) is required

The customer will be responsible for any additional costs incurred. For example, the estimated additional cost for lifting by a UNIC vehicle is about INR 5,000 to INR 10,000 (tax included).

  • * The amount may be higher than the standard amount depending on the installation location and the conditions of the loading / unloading route.
  • * The official amount will be announced by the delivery company at the time of delivery.
  • * Additional shipping charges will be paid directly to the vendor.

Route in your home

About picking up massage chairs that are no longer needed

Only when you purchase a massage chair from the online shop, we accept the collection of massage chairs that you no longer need, regardless of the manufacturer of the other company. For each purchase, you can pick up one for a fee (11,000 yen including tax). Customers who wish to pick up should select “I would like to pick up” when ordering. If you do not have the option to pick up the item, please enter “I would like to pick it up” in the inquiry field when ordering.

Request to customers who wish to pick up

“Massage chair model number you want to pick up (regardless of other manufacturers)”, “Massage chair installation location you want to pick up”, “Pick up” in the inquiry column on the “Confirmation of order details” page Please fill in as much as you can understand “the width of the desired massage chair”. At the time of product delivery, the delivery company will pick up the unnecessary massage chair.

Precautions for picking up unnecessary massage chairs

  • We do not accept pick-up only.
  • We do not accept collections other than massage chairs.
  • For an order for one massage chair, only one massage chair can be picked up.
  • In principle, we do not accept orders for more than one massage chair.

If you would like to pick up more than one, please contact us first. We will confirm whether the second and subsequent units can be picked up and estimate the pick-up fee individually. Customers are responsible for any additional charges. During the free pick-up campaign period, the first car will be handled free of charge, but the customer will be responsible for the fee for picking up the second and subsequent cars.

  • If the massage chair to be picked up is in a different location from the delivery address, we will not be able to handle it.
  • Customers are responsible for any additional charges incurred when increasing the number of staff or carrying out the unic when picking up unnecessary massage chairs. For details , please see ” About loading and unloading massage chairs “.
  • The delivery company does not support the disassembly work of the collected products. If you cannot carry it out in the assembled state, we ask you to disassemble it, so please check in advance if it can be disassembled.
